Caffeine sensitivity: practical adjustments

Sensitivity shows up as “caffeine hits hard” or “caffeine lasts too long.” The fix is usually timing first, then dose.

Based on research showing caffeine can affect sleep even 6 hours before bed
If you’re troubleshooting sleep, treat “6 hours” as a minimum starting point, then personalize with the calculators.

Common signs

Trouble falling asleep, lighter sleep, more anxiety, or feeling “wired but tired” can all suggest you need earlier timing.

Start with timing

Move caffeine earlier before you cut it out completely. Timing is the highest-leverage variable.

Then adjust dose

If timing isn’t enough, reduce dose or switch to smaller servings — especially later in the day.
