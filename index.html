Credibility, without medical claims

Based on research showing caffeine can affect sleep even 6 hours before bed
Many sleep resources recommend avoiding substantial caffeine within ~6 hours of bedtime, since it may still reduce sleep quality. This site provides educational estimates to help you plan, not diagnose or treat anything.
Is this accurate for everyone?

Caffeine metabolism varies a lot between people. That’s why each calculator lets you choose a sensitivity tier (half-life).

Why do you use half-life?

Half-life is a simple model for how quickly caffeine tends to decline. It’s a useful estimate for planning cutoff time.
